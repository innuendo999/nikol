Makeup Course Guide

Makeup Courses in Prague

Looking for a makeup course in Prague? Whether you want to finally master your own everyday look or take your first steps as a professional, a personalised one-on-one lesson is the fastest way to learn techniques that actually work for your face.

Who are makeup classes in Prague for?

Makeup classes suit complete beginners, anyone preparing for a special occasion, and aspiring makeup artists. Because lessons are one-on-one, the content is adapted to your level – nobody is left behind and nobody is bored.

What does a makeup lesson cover?

A typical makeup lesson covers skin preparation, colour theory and face mapping, foundation and base, eye makeup and lashes, contour and highlight, and how to choose the right tools and products. You apply the makeup yourself, with guidance, so the skills stay with you.

One-on-one vs group makeup classes

Group classes can be fun, but a one-on-one makeup lesson means the entire session is built around your features, skin type and goals. For most people learning faster and remembering more, private lessons are the better value.

English-speaking makeup courses in Prague

For expats and international clients, learning in your own language matters. Nikol teaches in fluent English and Czech, so every step is clear and you can ask anything along the way.

What you'll be able to do afterwards

  • Everyday makeup – a quick, flattering look you can do confidently

  • Occasion makeup – a polished look for events, dates and photos

  • Professional technique – foundations for working as an MUA

  • Smart shopping – a product and tool list tailored to you

Tell Nikol what you want to achieve and she'll shape the course around it – from a single focused session to a fuller programme.

Frequently Asked Questions

Makeup Course Questions

  • 1. Who are the makeup courses suitable for?
    The courses suit everyone – complete beginners learning everyday makeup for themselves, people preparing for a special occasion, and aspiring makeup artists building professional skills. Each course is personalised to your level and goals.
  • 2. What does a makeup course cover?
    One-on-one sessions cover skin preparation, colour theory, foundation and base, eye makeup and lashes, contour and highlight, plus tool and product selection. We work on your own face so you can repeat the looks at home.
  • 3. Are lessons one-on-one or in a group?
    Lessons are personalised one-on-one sessions, so the full session focuses entirely on you. Small private group sessions can be arranged on request.
  • 4. Do I need to bring my own products?
    You can bring your existing products so we work with what you own and recommend improvements, or use professional products during the lesson. Afterwards you receive a personalised product and tool list.
  • 5. How long is a makeup course and what does it cost?
    Length and price depend on your level and goals – from a single focused lesson to a fuller programme. Tell Nikol what you'd like to learn and she'll send a personalised quote within 24 hours.
  • 6. Are the courses available in English?
    Yes. Nikol teaches in fluent English and Czech, making the courses comfortable for international clients and expats living in Prague.
