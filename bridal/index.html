Bridal Makeup in Prague

Your Perfect Wedding Look

Your wedding day is one of the most significant moments of your life, and my goal is to make you feel confident, radiant, and absolutely beautiful. As a professional bridal makeup artist in Prague, I specialize in creating long-lasting, photogenic looks that enhance your natural features while reflecting your personal style.

Whether you dream of a soft, romantic glow, a classic elegant look, or something modern and bold, we will work together to turn your vision into reality. I use only high-end, professional products (like MAC, Dior, Estee Lauder) to ensure your makeup withstands tears of joy, hugs, and dancing all night long.

1. The Trial Session

We meet 4-6 weeks before the wedding to discuss your dress, theme, and preferences. We'll test the look to ensure it's exactly what you want.

2. The Wedding Day

I provide on-site service at your hotel or home in Prague. Relax and enjoy being pampered while I ensure you look flawless.

Good to Know

FAQ

  • 1. Do I need a makeup trial?
    Yes, a trial is highly recommended. It allows us to get to know each other, discuss your vision, and test the products on your skin to ensure no allergies and perfect longevity. Ideally, schedule this 4-8 weeks before the wedding.
  • 2. Do you travel to the venue?
    Absolutely. On your wedding day, I will travel to your hotel, apartment, or wedding venue within Prague (or outside upon agreement) so you can relax and get ready in comfort.
  • 3. What brands do you use?
    I use professional luxury brands designed for high-definition photography and durability. My kit includes products from MAC, Charlotte Tilbury, Estee Lauder, Dior, and Natasha Denona.
  • 4. Can you do makeup for bridesmaids and guests?
    Yes, I can accommodate bridesmaids and family members. For larger groups, please let me know in advance so we can plan the timing accordingly.
