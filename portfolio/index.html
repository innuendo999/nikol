Makeup Portfolio

This is a curated selection of makeup work created by Nikol Pavlova, a professional makeup artist based in Prague, Czech Republic. The portfolio covers bridal looks for weddings across Prague and Europe, glamorous evening and cocktail makeup, soft natural nude styling, commercial and editorial work for photo shoots, and behind-the-scenes images from one-on-one makeup courses. Each look is designed to enhance the client's natural features while reflecting their personal style and the occasion.

Inside the Portfolio

About Each Category

Wedding Makeup

Bridal looks designed to last from the first kiss to the final dance. Each wedding service includes a pre-wedding trial in Prague, day-of application with long-wear products suited to Czech summer and winter conditions, individual lashes, and a personal touch-up kit. Available for ceremonies in Prague Old Town, Vinohrady, château weddings outside the city, and destination weddings across Europe.

Evening & Cocktail Makeup

Glamorous looks built for galas, cocktail parties, proms, balls and special events in Prague. Smoky eyes, sculpted cheekbones, statement lips and luminous skin — finished with techniques that hold up under both flash photography and ambient evening light.

Nude & Natural Makeup

Soft, skin-first looks that enhance natural features without looking heavy. Ideal for engagement shoots, daytime portraits, civil ceremonies and clients who prefer a barely-there finish with healthy glowing skin.

Commercial & Editorial

Makeup for photo shoots, brand campaigns, lookbooks, magazine editorials and video content. Adapted to the lighting and concept of each shoot — from clean beauty work to bold editorial styling — with on-set touch-ups across the production day.

Personalized Makeup Courses

One-on-one makeup courses tailored to your level — from clients who want to master everyday looks to aspiring makeup artists building professional skills. Held in Prague, covering skin prep, color theory, application techniques, tool selection and product recommendations using your own face as the canvas.
